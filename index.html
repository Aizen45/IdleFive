HOW TO START



Presentation

First of all, hello to you.
I want to thank you for playing this little game that i created.
IdleFive is a GTA-Like game that aims to look like Grand Theft Auto V.
But, with a different vision and a different gameplay of the original game.


Goal

The main goal of the game is to get everything that you can buy (for now this is limited)
To do that you have to steal the money of people with your "Weapons".
You can also create your own business empire with the "Activities".


How to play ?

You can start by clicking on the fist to make some money.
After that you can buy a Weapon and/or start an Activity to gain a lot more money.
Buying an Activity will increase your rank and you need that to change your character (see character).

Weapons

Weapons are used to do more damage and rob the money of the people on the street,
there is also a random quality system that can raise or lower the power of the weapon.


Activities

This is the main mechanic of the game,
they are used to automatise the game and the more you have,
the more it costs you but that also allows you to have a lot of money without doing anything.


Character

This is the prestige system of the game.
Once you have the required money and rank,
you can change your character and get Character Points (CP)
It will cost all your Weapons, Money and Activities but you still keep your Vehicles.



Have fun,
- Aizen.
